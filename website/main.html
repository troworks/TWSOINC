TWSO ART AND ANIMATION DEPARTMENT INFO

the TWSO ART AND ANIMATION (or A&A) DEPARTMENT is a project made by the TWSO Company to introduce new art and cartoon shows to the world, like MackerVille, jaxton and andy, San and Pan And kiki land.

HISTORY

The A&A Project is made by the TWSO Company, by a man named "Zachary J Sanders" the Department Head of the company, "I Have Never Seen Any Cartoons As A Kid, I Would Not LIke Any Child To Expirience The Same Situation I Had" The Project Started In June 14 1934, Ended In January 6 1947, The First Cartoon Ever Aired On The Channel Called "MackerVille" Became An Instant Hit To KIds And History Is Made!
